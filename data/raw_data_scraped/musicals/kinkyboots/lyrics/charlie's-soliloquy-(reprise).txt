
Charlie's Soliloquy (Reprise)


Charlie:

Who was I kidding

This scheme was skidding

My fractured attempt at

Taking control.



I tried in vain, now

I'm to blame

Now I'm left with a

Deep dark hole.



So confident

So collected and so cool

Hey, look at me now

I'm a fool.
