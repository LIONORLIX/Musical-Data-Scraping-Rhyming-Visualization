
Make Way


Male Chorus:

Make way!

He comes!

His royal tallness

His highest highness

His way, way-upness

His mountainship!



Make way!

He comes!

King Arik.



Female Chorus:

Make way!

She comes!

Her regal proudness

Her flashy highness

Her self-indulgeness



All:

Her goddessness!



Make way!

She comes!

Princess Barbara.
