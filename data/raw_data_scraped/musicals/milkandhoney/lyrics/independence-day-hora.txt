
Independence Day Hora


Sing, sing a song of MAZELTOV,

Keep mazel at your side;

Sing, sing a song of MAZELTOV,

Toast the lucky groom!

Toast the happy bride!

Dance, dance the weary world away,

Now that the knot is tied;

Sing, sing a song of MAZELTOV,

The lucky groom!

The happy bride!
